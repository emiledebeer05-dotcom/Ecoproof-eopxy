A Branch of Ecoproof Africa

Ecoproof Epoxy is a proud division of Eco Proof Africa, established in 2010 by Jacques Du Plessis. With over 20 years of industry experience, Eco Proof Africa specializes in fully bonded, liquid-applied seamless waterproofing systems. Our epoxy division applies this same legacy of innovation and extreme durability to residential, commercial, and industrial flooring. Learn more at the Eco Proof Africa Website.
